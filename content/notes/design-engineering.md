Design engineering feels closest to me because it rewards both taste and implementation follow-through

It asks a person to think about:

- Building reusable components/primitives
- interaction quality
- implementation reality

The role feels healthiest when it reduces the distance between idea and interface.

Design Engineers contributes to branding, marketing, product development. This unique blend of work
