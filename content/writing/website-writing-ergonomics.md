Good writing tools make a few quiet promises.

They make starting easy, editing obvious, and publishing unsurprising.

For a personal site, ergonomics are not only about the editor. They also show up in the reading surface:

1. Comfortable line length
2. Gentle visual rhythm
3. Clear metadata
4. Links that still feel like part of the prose

I want this site to feel like a place where longer thoughts can sit without being rushed.
