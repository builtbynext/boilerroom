I want my personal website to feel quick in the hand, almost like pulling a notebook out of a bag and flipping it open.

- Keep the layout stable.
- Make navigation obvious.
- Let words do the heavy lifting.

The site does not need to feel sparse or cold. It just needs to get out of the way fast enough that reading can begin.

## Why I Keep Returning To Simple Structures

The older I get, the more I like interfaces that can survive long periods of neglect.

If I disappear for a month and return with a new essay, a book note, and three small scraps of thought, the system should still make sense. That is the kind of durability I want from a personal archive.
