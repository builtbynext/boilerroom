One of the quiet skills in design is translating intuition into reasons.

This book is useful because it treats communication as part of the job, not as a final polishing step after the real work is done.

## What Stuck

- Explain tradeoffs, not just outputs.
- Anchor decisions in goals and constraints.
- Make collaboration easier by reducing ambiguity early.
