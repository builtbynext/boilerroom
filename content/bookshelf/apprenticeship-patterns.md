This book feels like advice from people who respect both the work and the worker.

## Notes I Want To Keep

> Find mentors, but also become the sort of person who can be mentored well.

- Feedback loops matter because memory is unreliable.
- Concrete skills buy confidence.
- Sharing what you learn is part of learning.

I keep returning to the idea that craft is not only talent plus taste. It is also cadence, repetition, and reflection.
